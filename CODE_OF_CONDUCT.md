# Code of Conduct

Hostwright contributors are expected to keep discussions technical, respectful, and focused on building reliable infrastructure software.

## Standards

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Ground technical claims in evidence.
- Separate product ambition from supported behavior.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory conduct.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove disruptive contributions or discussions that make the project less safe, less accurate, or less maintainable.
